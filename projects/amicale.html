Platform Dedicated to the Management of Services and Activities of Companies’ Social Clubs

Context & Objective

This project aims to digitize and automate the management of companies’ social clubs, which traditionally relied on paper documents and Excel files. The objective was to design a modern, web-based platform that centralizes operations and improves overall efficiency, transparency, and data reliability.

Problem Statement

The analysis of existing systems highlighted several limitations:

  • Heavy reliance on paper-based documentation
  • Lack of automation and operational traceability
  • Time-consuming and error-prone manual processes
  • Difficulty tracking members, suppliers, and financial operations

Proposed Solution

To address these challenges, a dynamic web application was developed to centralize and automate key management processes, including:

  • Member and administrator management
  • Supplier and contract management
  • Event and special offer management
  • Publication and gallery content management
  • User account creation and secure authentication
  • Profile settings and multi-language support
  • Financial tracking and reporting
