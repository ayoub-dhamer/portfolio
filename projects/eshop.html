Electronics Shop & Repair Management Mobile Application

Context & Objective

This mobile application was developed for a small electronics retail shop to digitalize both product sales and repair services within a single platform. The app provides a seamless experience for customers to browse and purchase electronics while allowing them to track the repair status of their personal devices handled by the shop.

Objectives

  • Simplify the management of an electronics shop through a mobile platform
  • Provide customers with real-time visibility into their repair requests
  • Centralize product sales, customer accounts, and repair tracking
  • Improve customer experience and operational efficiency for the shop owner
  • Implement role-based access with dedicated features for Customers and the Shop Owner

Customer Features

Customers can create a personal account and access a complete set of features designed to simplify shopping and repair tracking.

User Authentication
  • Account creation and secure login
  • Profile management and personal information updates
Product Browsing & Shopping
  • Browse electronics products (PCs, smartphones, gaming accessories, peripherals)
  • Search products by name or category
  • View detailed product descriptions and prices
  • Add products to cart and complete purchases
Purchase Management
  • View purchase history
  • Track previous orders
Repair Tracking
  • View devices submitted for repair
  • Track repair status (pending, in progress, fixed)
  • Receive confirmation when a device is repaired

Shop Owner (Admin) Features

The shop owner has access to an administrative interface with full control over users, products, and repair services.

User Management
  • View the list of registered users
  • Delete user accounts when necessary
Product Management
  • Add new products to the shop
  • Update product details (price, description, availability)
  • Remove products from the catalog
Repair Management
  • Register customer devices for repair
  • Update repair status (not fixed, in progress, fixed)
  • Allow customers to track repair progress in real time

Technical & Functional Highlights

  • Role-based access control (Customer / Admin)
  • Centralized database for users, products, purchases, and repairs
  • Real-time repair status updates visible to customers
  • Simple, intuitive, and mobile-friendly user interface
  • Architecture designed for small businesses with scalability in mind

Value & Impact

  • Reduces manual paperwork and operational overhead
  • Improves customer communication and transparency
  • Centralizes sales and repair management in one system
  • Provides a professional and modern experience for customers
