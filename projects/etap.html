Platform Dedicated to the Management of all the Services and Activitivities of ETAP (Entreprise Tunisienne d’Activités Pétrolières)

Context & Objective

The objective of this project was to design and implement a dynamic web application aimed at automating and digitizing ETAP’s administrative and operational processes, with a particular focus on client management, service orders, and document handling.

Company Context

ETAP (Entreprise Tunisienne des Activités Pétrolières) is the national entity responsible for managing Tunisia’s petroleum and natural gas resources. Its core activities include:

  • Exploration and production of oil and gas resources
  • Promotion and development of hydrocarbon projects
  • Management of technical and economic studies
  • Supply of gas and crude oil to the national market

Prior to this project, ETAP relied heavily on paper-based workflows for managing client records, service orders, and document approvals. This traditional approach resulted in operational inefficiencies, processing delays, and increased risks related to document loss and data inconsistency.

Problem Statement

The analysis of the existing system revealed several critical issues:

  • Excessive reliance on paper-based documentation
  • High risk of document loss and data inconsistency
  • Limited accessibility to archived records
  • Time-consuming and error-prone manual workflows

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution consists of a secure, web-based management system designed to automate client handling, service order processing, digital signatures, and document tracking. The platform streamlines administrative operations while ensuring improved data reliability, faster processing times, and enhanced traceability across all workflows.

Technologies Used
