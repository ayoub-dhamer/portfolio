Saleti – Complete Islamic Companion App

Overview

Saleti is a Flutter-based Islamic lifestyle app designed for spiritual organization, Qur’an tracking, and prayer guidance. It combines practical tools like prayer reminders, a Hijri calendar, Qibla finder, and a personal Du’a journal with interactive tracking features like Surah goals, progress graphs, and Khatm tracking.

🌙 Prayer Times & Notifications

Automatically calculates prayer times based on your GPS location, provides reminders and Azan notifications.

User Perspective

  • Silent reminders or full Azan notifications
  • Adjust notification time before prayer
  • Test Azan feature to preview prayer sounds

Technical Perspective

  • AndroidAlarmManager schedules background alarms
  • FlutterLocalNotificationsPlugin handles notifications
  • MethodChannel integration for native Azan audio
  • Customizable volume and enable/disable options

📅 Hijri Calendar

Displays a full Islamic calendar with all major Muslim holidays.

User Perspective

  • Navigate months and tap dates for holiday details
  • Marks Ramadan, Eid, Ashura, and other key events

Technical Perspective

  • Calendar data stored locally or calculated dynamically
  • Custom widgets highlight special dates with icons/colors

🧭 Qibla Finder

Shows precise direction to Mecca using your device compass and GPS.

Technical Perspective

  • Integrates device compass sensors
  • Calculates Qibla angle based on GPS coordinates
  • Smooth rotation animations for real-time orientation

📖 Mushaf & Qur’an Reading

Access all Surahs, bookmark pages, set Surah goals, and track Khatm progress with graphs.

Technical Perspective

  • Persistent storage tracks bookmarks, Khatm, and Surah goals
  • PageView.builder for navigation through Mushaf
  • Charts visualize reading stats over time
  • Dynamic progress updates for Surah goals

📝 Du’a Journal

Store favorite Du’as in Arabic or transliteration, read them anytime, with gallery swipe mode and adjustable font.

Technical Perspective

  • Auto-size text ensures readability on all devices
  • Persistent storage keeps Du’as and font settings
  • Animated hold-to-delete prevents accidental removal
  • Wakelock integration keeps screen active in gallery mode

💡 Technical & UI Highlights

  • Elegant UI/UX with gradients, cards, shadows, and responsive layout
  • Flutter-based cross-platform app (Android & iOS)
  • Local storage ensures offline data persistence
  • Integrates native services for Azan playback
  • Animations and gestures for smooth interactions
  • Right-to-left support and Arabic fonts (Amiri)
