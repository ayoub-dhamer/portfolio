Web Solution for Salon Operations and Services

Context & Objective

This project focuses on the design and development of a dynamic web application for managing the operations of a beauty care center. The solution was created to address the limitations of the existing system (“YourCegid”) by offering a more flexible, modern, and user-friendly platform.

The application enables clients to make online reservations and purchases, while administrators (assistants) can efficiently manage services, products, staff, resources, and appointments through a centralized interface.

Objectives

  • Automate and centralize beauty care center management
  • Provide clients with an online booking and shopping platform
  • Improve resource management (employees, rooms, and equipment)
  • Integrate billing, reporting, and customer communication

Key Features

Client Side
  • Account creation and profile management
  • Browsing services and product catalogs
  • Online appointment booking and purchases
  • Shopping cart management
  • Messaging system to communicate with administrators
Admin Side (Assistant)
  • Manage clients, staff members, services, and products
  • Manage equipment and room availability
  • Track reservations, appointments, and billing
  • Monitor availability and update service statuses
  • Generate reports and performance insights
